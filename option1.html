Curriculum

What you will master

10 months of hands-on training across every modern journalism platform — from fundamentals to specialization.

01

Print & Digital Journalism

News writing, features, editing, subediting, and publishing — for print and digital platforms. Produce ACJ's twice-weekly newspaper throughout the year.

02

Broadcast & TV Journalism

Anchoring, scripting, video production, camera operation, and live studio production in a fully equipped broadcast environment.

03

Radio Journalism

Produce radio news programmes in professional recording studios. Audio editing, scripting, and on-air presentation techniques.

04

Data & AI Journalism

Use data tools, AI, and visual storytelling to produce evidence-based journalism. Work with datasets, infographics, and AI-assisted reporting workflows.

05

Covering Deprivation — Signature Module

ACJ's flagship course. Report on marginalized communities with the depth, sensitivity, and accuracy their stories demand.

06

Entrepreneurial Journalism

Build and monetize your own media platform. Learn to sustain yourself as an independent journalist or launch your own media venture.
